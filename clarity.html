Master the Piano with Expert Instruction

My piano lessons are designed to nurture your musical journey, whether you're taking your first steps at the keyboard or looking to refine advanced techniques. I believe every student has unique potential, and my teaching approach adapts to your individual learning style, goals, and musical interests to help you flourish as a pianist.

What you'll learn in piano lessons:

  • Proper hand position, posture, and fundamental piano technique
  • Basic music theory as it applies to piano playing
  • Reading music notation and developing sight-reading skills
  • Classical repertoire from Bach to modern masters
  • Popular music and your favourite songs
  • Performance skills, ABRSM exam preparation, and confidence building
  • Creative musical exploration
