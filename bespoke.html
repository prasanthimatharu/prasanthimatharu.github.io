Designing Excellence Your Way

The Bespoke coaching programme is designed to assist you in achieving your specific goal(s).

We start with an in-depth consultation to map out your current situation, your long term goals and put in place some milestone goals.

Regular sessions feature:

  • A review of your progress to higlight successes and challenges helping you stay on track.
  • Goal setting.
  • Values elicitiation.
  • Brainstorming sessions where I'll challenge you to go beyond your comfort zone to see what opportunities a new perspective presents to you.
  • Action planning.
  • Accountability and motivation.
