Unlock the Language of Music Through Theory

Understanding music theory is like learning the language that connects all musicians. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced player looking to deepen your understanding, my music theory lessons provide the foundation you need to become a more confident and well-rounded musician.

Music theory doesn't have to be intimidating or dry. I make it accessible and practical, connecting theoretical concepts directly to the music you love and play. You'll discover how theory enhances your musical expression, improves your sight-reading, and opens up new creative possibilities.

In music theory lessons, you'll learn:

  • Note names, staff notation, and clef reading
  • Scales, modes, and key signatures
  • Intervals, chords, and chord progressions
  • Time signatures, rhythm, and meter
  • Circle of fifths and harmonic relationships
  • Ear training and interval recognition
  • Analysis of musical forms and structures
  • Composition techniques and creative application
  • ABRSM theory exam preparation and grading requirements
