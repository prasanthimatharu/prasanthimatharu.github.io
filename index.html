Why Learn Piano & Violin
With Me?

  • You've always wanted to learn piano or violin but never knew where to start.
  • You're looking for personalized instruction tailored to your learning style and goals.
  • You want to develop proper technique from the beginning or improve existing skills.
  • You're interested in exploring different musical genres and styles.

What Makes My Teaching Special?

  • Personalized lesson plans designed around your musical interests and learning pace.
  • Strong foundation in proper technique combined with creative musical exploration.
  • Experience teaching students of all ages, from young children to adult beginners.
  • Regular progress assessments and performance opportunities to build confidence.
  • Flexible scheduling and supportive learning environment.
  • Integration of music theory, ear training, and practical playing skills.
  • Focus on developing your unique musical voice and expression.
